About leja

This is a simple USSD application that takes book keeping to a whole new level .

Leja is an inventory keeping USSD that assists the small and medium enterprise owners book keep efficiently. It was inspired by the number of SMEs that closed shop over the past few numbers due to mismanagement of funds and poor book keeping. According to a survey done by datasoft it shows that close to 200,00 SMEs annually.This way we saw it wise to develop a simple system that will help the business owners effeciently and effectively conduct business.
